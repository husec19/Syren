 Usage: Syren(.exe) -file <full pathname to file>, -plist <directory>, -url <url:port>

 Options:
	   
  -file audiofile.(-.M4A, -.MP3, -.WMA) -> exp: Syren -file obeobsolete.m4a

  -plist <directory name> -> exp: Syren -plist C:\\myplaylist  

  -stream <url> (WMA streaming stations only) -> exp: Syren -url http://live1.kexp.org:80
